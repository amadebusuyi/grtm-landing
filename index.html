Get to Know Us

...

GRTM Summer Academy was set up by the Glory Realms Teens Ministry to help teenagers master the skills necessary to own the future.

The Academy teaches you a core skill in either Arts or Tech as well as other necessary soft skills like Leadership, Communication, Emotional Intelligence and others necessary to thrive in today's fast paced work environment. There is also a School of Godliness which features courses on Service, Purpose, Passion, Integrity and Faith.

Join the Summer Academy as it runs from 25th July to 2nd September, 2022. Classes will be held on Mondays to Fridays from 10am to 2pm.
